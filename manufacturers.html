The New York Blower Company

The New York Blower Company designs and builds fans and blowers to move air in all types of commercial and industrial applications. Today, NYB has one of the most complete portfolios in the world, consisting of literally thousands of different types, models, and sizes of air-movement equipment.

By carrying the broadest product line in the business, NYB is able to engineer individual, customized solutions that meet the specific requirements of even the most demanding industrial and commercial applications. Range includes fans and blowers for special requirements, such as temperatures to 1300*F, pressures to 128" WG, and flows to 350,000 CFM. A variety of materials are available, including fiberglass, aluminum, stainless steel, and hot-dip galvanizing, along with numerous special coatings.

NYB Heavy Industrial Custom Products and Services

The New York Blower Company is a full service fan manufacturer offering a complete array of centrifugal and axial products. These range from standard, predesigned fans to highly customized products. NYB excels at designing and constructing fans for corrosive, abrasive and high temperature applications (beyond 1800°F.). In addition, NYB can modify fans to meet specific application needs such as zero leakage construction, abrupt temperature change, cyclical speed changes and more. NYB can repair, modify, and replace all fan manufacturer's designs and components. Range includes fans and blowers with pressures to 150" w.g. and flows over 1,000,000 CFM.

Spencer Turbine Company

Spencer offers a wide range of blower types from single stage centrifugal and regenerative models to multistage centrifugals with fabricated or cast construction. Power Mizer® blowers with cast iron and aluminum components have very high operating efficiency suited for continuous-duty service, while fabricated blowers of carbon steel allow substitution of specialized metals for precise resistance to abrasion, corrosion or extreme temperatures. Overall performance maximums: 50,000 ICFM, 20 PSIG pressure, 15" Hg vacuum.

From single-user, mobile vacuum cleaning units to extensive, multi-operator central vacuum systems, Spencer has standard or custom designs for all industrial, commercial and institutional facilities. Besides heavy-duty vacuum cleaning, Spencer systems can perform high-velocity dust collection, pneumatic conveying, liquid pickup, scrap removal, hospital sanitizing and similar intensive applications, using specialized collectors, separators, and filtration systems.
